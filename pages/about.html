ABOUT ME

I'm Irene, a passionate graphic designer dedicated to bringing your creative visions to life. I've worked on email marketing since I first started to work as a professional and have experience working with Klaviyo, MailChimp and designing directly for HTML.

I'm passionate about making your email dreams come true. To make sure we're a great match, here's a few things I look for:

I'M THE DESIGNER FOR YOU IF

  • You know what you want and know your brand like your own child and need someone to bring your ideas to life.

  • The ideal client is respectful of my time and skills, and values the design process.

  • You understand that the best results are achieved by a process of collaboration and with some design freedom.

If these points sound true to you, let's make beautiful work together!
